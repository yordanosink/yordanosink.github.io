Untitled

The quiet hum of evening light,
A whisper soft, a fading sight.

Words dissolve like drifting air,
Leaving only silence there.

On Silence

Silence is not the absence of sound, but the presence of something deeper.

We often misunderstand it, filling it quickly, fearing what it might reveal. But silence, when allowed, becomes a mirror.

A Small Room

A: Do you hear that?

B: Hear what?

A: Exactly.
